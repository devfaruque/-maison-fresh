Our Story

Maison Fresh brings together the sophistication of Fortnum & Mason with the authentic charm of Toronto's beloved Summerhill Market. We're creating a premium destination for discerning food lovers who appreciate quality, sustainability, and artisanal craftsmanship.

Located in the heart of St. Lawrence Market's Upper Level, we curate the finest organic produce, innovative vegan cuisine, and artisan grocery items from local producers and international purveyors.
